Decision Framework

Option A: The Consolidated Master Pool

  • PRO: Operational Simplicity. One account, one login, and one tax form to track at the end of the year.
  • PRO: Total Visibility. Seeing $47k+ in one balance provides a high level of "Emergency Security."
  • CON: Yield Lock. If another bank offers a higher rate (4.25%+) on the $25k, you lose that extra income by staying 100% in Amex.

Option B: The Dual-Track Strategy

  • PRO: Yield Optimization. Capturing a higher rate on the Booster account translates to roughly $750 - $1,000 in extra "free" cash.
  • PRO: Psychological Moat. The "Gold Line" (Savings) is never touched by the car payment, keeping it emotionally separate.
  • CON: Management Overhead. Requires checking two different apps and managing transfers between banks if needed.
