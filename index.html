Option A: The "Single Hub"

Combine all $47,000 into a single Amex HYSA at 3.25% APY.

  • Pros: Ultimate simplicity. One login, one 1099-INT tax form, and a massive "Snowball" effect as interest builds on the larger balance.
  • Cons: You are capped at Amex's rate. You lose out on the "yield spread" offered by more aggressive online banks.

Option B: The "Dual Track"

Keep $22k in Amex (Hub) and $25k in a high-yield hunter (e.g., Wealthfront) at ~4.25% APY.

  • Pros: Better math. That extra 1% on your $25k savings adds roughly $750+ in pure profit over the 36 months.
  • Cons: Slightly higher "mental load." You have to track two separate accounts and ensure the Hub never runs dry.
